## Case study cards

### Aquaporo
**Atmospheric water harvesting** · **Engaged at pre-seed** · **3 provisionals, 2 non-provisionals**

A pre-seed deep tech company building an energy-efficient alternative to desalination. We filed three provisionals in quick succession before their seed raise, supported diligence calls with their corporate VC, and received allowable subject matter on both non-provisionals at conversion.

### BikeOn
**E-mobility & micromobility** · **Engaged pre-launch** · **International filing + portfolio takeover**

A consumer e-mobility company building bicycle electrification products manufactured in China. We filed an international application on a complex mechanical device in time for a public Kickstarter launch, and took over additional cases from previous counsel.

[Read the case study →](/case-studies/bikeon)

### Kawa
**Sustainable food & ingredients** · **Engaged at pre-seed** · **Quick allowance, broad claim scope**

A pre-seed startup turning spent coffee grounds into a cocoa substitute. We educated a first-time inventor team on the patent process and focused the application on the flavoring methods that actually constituted the moat. Result: a quick allowance with relatively broad claim scope.

[Read the case study →](/case-studies/kawa)

---

### Scaled Carbon
**Ocean MRV for carbon removal** · **Engaged at early stage** · **PCT with positive ISR**

An early-stage carbon sequestration company building MRV technology for ocean enhanced weathering. We drafted a comprehensive international application on a small budget. The PCT received an entirely positive international search report. Scaled Carbon is now preparing for national-phase entry in multiple countries.

[Read the case study →](/case-studies/scaled-carbon)

### Ulysses
**Maritime robotics & physical AI** · **Engaged at seed** · **3 application families, $38M Series A**

A Lowercarbon-backed maritime robotics company building underwater autonomous drone fleets for ecosystem restoration. We built a portfolio across three application families, including two international applications. Both PCTs received positive international search reports. Ulysses closed a $38M Series A from Andreessen Horowitz.
